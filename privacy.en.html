Stickee Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-01

Stickee (the "Service") respects your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and retain it, and how we dispose of it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Apple Sign-In data: your Apple user identifier and email address (Apple Private Relay supported)
  • Profile: nickname; optional profile photo
  • User content: text posts (up to 200 characters), attached images, 1:1 direct messages, replies (chain notes)
  • Location: latitude/longitude at the moment a post is created (full precision)
  • Automatically collected: session token, IP address, device information (app version, OS version)

We do NOT collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Contacts, calendar, health data, or payment information

2. Purposes

  • Account authentication via Apple Sign-In
  • Showing posts from nearby users (distance is computed from location)
  • Sending and receiving 1:1 direct messages
  • Handling reports of objectionable content and content moderation
  • Service reliability (error tracking, abuse prevention)
  • Location coordinates of posts are delivered to other clients within the same area (a few kilometers) so each client can compute distance and place posts on the canvas. The raw coordinates themselves are not displayed in the UI.

3. Retention

Item Retention period
Account information Permanently deleted upon account deletion
Posts Automatically deleted 24 hours after creation (user may delete sooner)
Direct messages For the lifetime of the user's account
Reports 90 days, for moderation review

We do not retain any items required by statute.

4. Third Parties and Processors

  • Apple Inc. — handles Apple Sign-In authentication (limited purpose)
  • Supabase Inc. — provides database, Realtime, Storage, and Auth services (US region; exact region to be confirmed by operator and reflected here)

We do not share data with advertising or marketing/analytics services as of v1.0. Any change will be announced in advance.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: view your nickname and email in the Profile screen
  • Correction: edit your nickname
  • Deletion: Profile → Delete Account (permanent and irreversible)
  • Withdrawal of consent: deletion suffices
  • Suspension of processing: account deletion or sign out

6. Children Under 14

We recommend the Service for users 17 years and older (App Store age rating 17+). Accounts confirmed to belong to a person under 14 will be deleted immediately.

7. Security and Disposal

Security:

  • In transit: HTTPS (Supabase endpoints)
  • At rest: server-side encryption on Supabase (AES-256)
  • Access control: Supabase Row Level Security enforces per-user isolation
  • Storage region: Supabase US region

Disposal:

  • Account, content, DMs, reports: permanently deleted from the database (SQL DELETE) upon account deletion or retention expiry; not recoverable. Cascading foreign keys remove the user ID from all related tables in the same transaction.
  • Image files: removed via Supabase Storage delete API.
  • Backups: removed from backup snapshots on the regular backup rotation.
